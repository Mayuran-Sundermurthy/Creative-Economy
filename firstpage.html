What is creative economy?



Creative economy is the creative activities which economies are based on. Essentially, it is turning the creativity you have into an actual business. The 'creative industries' are career paths like arts and crafts, design, photography, music, dance, electronics, the list goes on and on. All of the proffessions that involve some kind of creativity and originality are considered creative industries. These creative industries are now some of the best sectors in the economy and are doing very well. Creative economy is all of these sectors combined to create an economy of creativity.
