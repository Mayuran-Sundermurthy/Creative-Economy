How can creativity help?



Creativity helps in lots of ways. Creative people give more ideas and solutions that someone with a closed mind that only uses solutions that have been tried and tested but creative people have newer solutions that might work better. Creativity provides more innovation and stimulates the mind further than old solutions. It also lets you express yourself through your ideas. Creativity helps in all of these ways, these ways can also be used to solve bigger problems and help the world further.
